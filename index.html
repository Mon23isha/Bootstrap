Wandoor Beach

Located 25km southwest of Port Blair, Wandoor Beach is known for its colorful corals and thickets. It's a popular place for tourists to relax and swim

Radhanagar Beach

It's known as one of the most beautiful beaches in Asia, and was named “Best Beach in Asia” by Time in 2004.

Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose Island

Earlier known as Ross Island is a small island located 3km east of port Blair less than a square kilometer in size.

Popular Water sports activities

Scuba Diving

Scuba Diving is a water sport that involves breathing air from a tank while underwater,You get to wittness the underwater life. helps to relax under silence of sea, far away from noise of civilization.

Snorkeling

Snorkeling is the activity of swimming underwater while breathing through a tube called a snorkel. It's a popular activity in warm, calm, clear oceans.

Parasailing

A water sport in which a person is towed behind a boat while attached to a parachute-like canopy. The parasail is suspended from a harness that is attached to the boat, and as the boat moves, the parasailer is lifted into the air and glides through the sky.
